Our Story

About Tiny Trekkers

Tiny Trekkers is a Pune-based adventure trekking company founded by a group of passionate hikers who fell in love with the Sahyadri mountains. What started as weekend trips with friends turned into a mission — to make the mountains accessible, safe, and joyful for everyone.

We are not just a trekking company. We are your trail companions — guiding first-timers and experienced hikers alike through Maharashtra's most stunning landscapes, with a commitment to safety, sustainability, and small group experiences that let you truly connect with nature.

Every trek is led by certified, experienced guides. Every route is safety-scouted. Every meal is prepared fresh on trail. We handle the logistics so you can focus entirely on the experience.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change unexpectedly. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our trekkers and our team, who plan logistics well in advance.

  • 7 or more days before trek date: Full refund — 100% of the amount paid.
  • 3 to 6 days before trek date: 50% refund of the amount paid.
  • Less than 3 days before trek date: No refund. However, you may transfer your slot to another person at no extra charge.
  • No-show on the day: No refund will be issued for no-shows without prior notice.

Refund Policy

All approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

  • Refunds are initiated only after a written cancellation request is received via WhatsApp or email.
  • Refunds are not applicable for treks cancelled by the trekker due to personal health, weather fears, or change of plans within 3 days.
  • If Tiny Trekkers cancels a trek due to adverse weather, safety concerns, or unavoidable circumstances, a full refund or free rescheduling will be provided.
  • Token amounts paid during enquiry are non-refundable if the booking is cancelled without 7 days' notice.

Trek Rescheduling

You may reschedule your booking to a future date with no additional charge, provided you notify us at least 3 days in advance. Rescheduling is subject to slot availability.
