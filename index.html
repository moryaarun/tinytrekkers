Our Story

About Tiny Trekkers

Tiny Trekkers is a Pune-based adventure trekking company founded by a group of passionate hikers who fell in love with the Sahyadri mountains. What started as weekend trips with friends turned into a mission — to make the mountains accessible, safe, and joyful for everyone.

We are your trail companions — guiding first-timers and experienced hikers alike through Maharashtra's most stunning landscapes, with a commitment to safety, sustainability, and small-group experiences.

Every trek is led by certified guides. Every route is safety-scouted. Every meal is prepared fresh on trail. We handle the logistics so you can focus entirely on the experience.

Cancellation Policy

  • 7 or more days before trek date: Full refund — 100% of the amount paid.
  • 3 to 6 days before trek date: 50% refund of the amount paid.
  • Less than 3 days before trek date: No refund. You may transfer your slot to another person.
  • No-show on the day: No refund issued without prior notice.
  • Advance payments: Advance booking fees are non-refundable within 3 days of the trek date.

Refund Policy

  • All approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.
  • If Tiny Trekkers cancels a trek, a full refund or free rescheduling is provided.
  • Token/advance amounts are non-refundable if cancelled within 3 days of the trek date.

Rescheduling

You may reschedule to a future date with no charge if you notify us at least 3 days in advance. Subject to slot availability.
