Who We Are

Born in Pune,
Raised in the Hills

Tiny Trekkers started as a group of passionate hikers from Pune who believed that the Sahyadri mountains deserved to be experienced — not just seen. We became a business so more people could safely discover what we love.

Every trek we design is led by certified guides, supported by medical-trained trek leaders, and built with your safety and wonder as the two non-negotiables.
